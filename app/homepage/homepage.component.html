About Me

Hi, I’m Derek Watters. My entire life revolves around design (don’t tell my wife). As long as I can remember, I’ve been designing things. This love has lead me down the path of many different passions, including woodworking, fabricating, web design/development, and even a career in Computer Aided Drafting and Design. I’ve designed things from furniture to distribution transformers, and many other machines in between. My love for designing things has always pushed me to understand the world around me and the engineering behind everything I use, including websites. I originally played with HTML and CSS as a teen, and even though I put that interest on the back burner while pursuing a degree in Computer Aided Design, it's always been something I’ve had a passion for. Below I’ve compiled a list of languages and programs I’ve learned over the years, along with a some projects I’ve made as well. I love new projects and the different challenges they bring, and would love to build something great with you.
